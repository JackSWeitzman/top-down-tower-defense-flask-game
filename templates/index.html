HOW TO PLAY

You will spawn in the center of the map

You will be able to move with w, a, s, d

You aim where to shoot with your mouse and the game will automatically shoot after an interval

You must defend yourself and your building in the middle from the enemies

When the building's health reaches 0, you will no longer get power-ups

When your health reaches 0, the game will end

Last as long as possible and get the highest score on the scoreboard. Good luck!
